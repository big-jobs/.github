# Building the Future of Hiring & Sales Intelligence

We are a team of engineers, data scientists, and product builders creating AI-native platforms that transform how companies hire and how sales teams qualify leads.

---

## Our Products

### [Origa](https://origa.io) — Enterprise-Grade Voice AI for Complex Sales

An AI concierge layer for high-ticket sales — voice, chat, WhatsApp, one memory. Origa deploys AI agents trained on 1M+ real sales conversations to conduct automated discovery calls, qualify leads, and route warm opportunities to sales teams before a human ever picks up the phone.

**What we do:**
- Batch calling at scale (thousands of parallel calls per hour)
- Real-time CRM enrichment and instant appointment booking
- Live call transfer to sales reps when a lead is hot
- Multi-channel outreach: voice, SMS, WhatsApp, email
- 100+ language support with mid-conversation switching
- Sub-800ms latency, <0.1% error rate

> "The AI concierge layer for high-ticket sales" — serving real estate, financial services, education, and recruitment across India, UAE, Singapore, and the US

**Founded:** 2024 · **HQ:** Dubai, UAE & Palo Alto, CA · **Backed by:** Antler Singapore

---

### [Big.Jobs](https://big.jobs) — Free-Forever ATS for Startup Hiring

An all-in-one talent suite built specifically for early-stage startups. Big.Jobs combines a permanently free applicant tracking system with on-demand recruiting services, making world-class hiring accessible to every growing team.

**What we do:**
- AI-powered candidate matching and ranking
- Customizable career sites with no code required
- Multi-job board publishing from a single dashboard
- Interview scheduling, offer management, and pre-boarding workflows
- Employee referral programs and Slack integration
- Optional on-demand recruiter subscription

> "Forever-free ATS for startup hiring" — trusted by 50+ of the hottest startups from SF to Singapore

**Founded:** 2019 · **HQ:** Palo Alto, CA & Bengaluru, India
